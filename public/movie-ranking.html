ALIE'S JANUARY 2024 MOVIE WATCH LIST

TOP THREE (3) MOVIES

1. KILLERS OF THE FLOWER MOON

2023 . Crime/Western . 3hrs 26mins

It is a 3 hours + movie starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Robert De Niro and directed by Martin Scorsese. It is based on a true story with stellar cast, cinematic excellence, compelling storytelling and a wonderful musical score. Despite the long duration, I promise it is worth watching.

2. SALTBURN

2023 . Thriller/Comedy . 2hrs 7mins

Saltburn is a spectacularly crafted masterpiece, a paced, and performed satire of wealth and extravegance...that simultaneously works to slowly peelback of the layers of each character scene by scene, until the audience is fully aware of what is happenning - but cannot begin to look away as the proverbial train car goes flying off the tracks.

3. FOE

2023 . Thriller/Sci-Fi . 1hr 48mins

I was drawn in straightaway as the suspense was 10/10. Then I was blown away when it all fell together. A quiet movie for a quiet night in. The movie is a thought provoking and eerily dystopian look at the possible uses for synthetic AI.
